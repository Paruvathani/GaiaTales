Giatales: A Climate Story

Making climate change data accessible to everyone

Project Overview

Giatales is an interactive platform that uses data from NASA and the US GHG Center to present a compelling story about climate change. Our aim is to educate a general audience and motivate action through accessible data visualizations and narratives.
